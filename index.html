OUR GAMES
Golem Pang

Deep within the mines lie long-forgotten golems, each guarding various gems. Maneuver through the pillars by blasting rocks to awaken the golems and make your escape from the cave. Gather dynamites to rouse new golems! Discover and collect 18 types of golems, including basalt, granite, limestone, and marble golems. Complete your rock box collection!

Pawpaw Pang

Vegetables, fruits, and fish are flying toward the cat, but it seems the cat isn't fond of vegetables and fruits. Each time your cat swallows a fish, you'll score points. Upon reaching a score of 100 hearts, you'll unlock a new adorable cat. Let's meet all the cats and complete our 'Pawpaw Pang Friends' collection. What kinds of cats are waiting for you?
